### Full Description:
```
Dabba Halifax brings authentic, home-style Indian tiffin meals right to your doorstep in Halifax, Nova Scotia.

🍱 FEATURES:

• WEEKLY MENU - Browse delicious rotating menus featuring traditional Indian dishes prepared fresh daily

• FLEXIBLE SUBSCRIPTIONS - Choose from multiple meal plans that fit your lifestyle and dietary preferences

• SPICE CUSTOMIZATION - Adjust spice levels (Mild, Medium, Spicy) to match your taste

• SKIP MEALS ANYTIME - Going out? Skip meals easily and get wallet credits for future use

• REAL-TIME TRACKING - Know exactly when your dabba is arriving with live driver tracking

• MEAL RATINGS - Rate your meals with quick emoji feedback to help us improve

• WALLET SYSTEM - Earn credits when you skip meals, automatically applied to renewals

🏠 FOR KITCHEN OWNERS:
• Complete kitchen management dashboard
• Menu scheduling and dish management
• Customer preference tracking
• Order preparation lists
• Analytics and feedback insights

🚗 FOR DELIVERY DRIVERS:
• Optimized delivery routes
• Real-time order updates
• Easy delivery completion with photo proof

Experience the warmth of home-cooked Indian meals delivered fresh to your door. Join the Dabba Halifax family today!

Questions? Contact us at support@dabbahalifax.com
```

## 🚀 BUILD COMMANDS

```bash
# Install EAS CLI
npm install -g eas-cli

# Login to Expo
eas login

# Configure project
eas build:configure

# Build for iOS
eas build --platform ios

# Submit to App Store
eas submit --platform ios
```
