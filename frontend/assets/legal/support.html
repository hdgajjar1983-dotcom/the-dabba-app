📋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to The Dabba?
Download our app, create an account, and choose a subscription plan (Weekly or Monthly). Select your delivery address and you're all set to receive fresh Gujarati meals!
Can I skip a meal?
Yes! You can skip any meal through the app before the cutoff time (usually 10 AM for same-day dinner). When you skip, you'll receive ₹120 credit in your wallet.
What are the delivery hours?
Dinner is delivered between 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM daily. You'll receive a notification when your driver is on the way.
How do I change my delivery address?
Go to Profile → Edit Address in the app. Changes apply to your next delivery. For same-day changes, please contact support.
What if I'm not home during delivery?
Our driver will call you. If unreachable, we'll leave the meal at your door and take a photo proof of delivery. You'll receive the photo via notification.
Are the meals vegetarian?
Yes! All our meals are 100% vegetarian, prepared following traditional Gujarati recipes with fresh ingredients.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Profile → Manage Subscription → Cancel. Refunds for unused days will be credited to your wallet within 3-5 business days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and wallet balance. All payments are processed securely.

⚠️ Urgent Issues?

For delivery emergencies or food safety concerns, please call us immediately at +1 (902) 555-1234. We prioritize urgent matters.
