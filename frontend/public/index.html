Our Story

The Dabba Heritage

Born from a passion for authentic Indian home cooking, Tha Dabba brings the warmth and love of a traditional Indian kitchen to Halifax. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, perfected over decades, and now delivered fresh to your doorstep.

Every meal is prepared with the finest ingredients, traditional spices imported directly from India, and the same care your grandmother would put into cooking for her family.
